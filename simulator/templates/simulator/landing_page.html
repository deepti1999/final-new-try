100ProSim is a comprehensive simulation tool that models Germany's path to 100% renewable energy by 2045. It calculates how changes in land use, renewable generation, and demand affect the energy balance, helping identify strategic pathways to replace fossil fuels with clean energy sources entirely.

How to navigate
A 3-step path so you know exactly where to click first.
1) Review data

Open the Renewable Data table to check targets vs. status and formulas. Adjust only targets; status stays as-is.

2) Balance WS storage

Go to the Annual Electricity diagram and click “Balance WS Storage”. This writes WS-backed targets (9.3.1/9.3.4) and cascades dependents.

3) Verify results

Refresh the Renewable Data table and the Annual Electricity view to see updated 9.x and 10.x targets and the final flows.

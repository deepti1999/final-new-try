Quick Start Guide

Follow these steps to get oriented. Each card links to the live page; the visuals hint at what you’ll see.

1) Annual Electricity

Open the diagram, review flows, and click “Balance WS Storage” to push WS-backed targets (9.3.1 / 9.3.4) and cascade dependents.

Open Annual Electricity
2) Renewable Data

Inspect status/target values and formulas. Targets update after WS balance; status stays as-is. Use this table to verify cascaded values (9.x, 10.x).

Open Renewable Data
3) Dashboard

Jump to the main dashboard to access land use, consumption, and quick links. Use it as your hub once you know the flow.

Open Dashboard
Tip: Typical sequence:
  • Open Annual Electricity → click “Balance WS Storage”.
  • Refresh Renewable Data to confirm 9.3.1 / 9.3.4 targets and dependent 9.x/10.x totals.
  • Use Dashboard for any additional navigation.
